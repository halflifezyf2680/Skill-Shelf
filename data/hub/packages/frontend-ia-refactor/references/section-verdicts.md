# Section Verdicts

Use these heuristics to decide section fate.

## Keep

- the section serves the page's primary task
- the section provides necessary context for the next decision

## Promote

- the section contains the main action or primary object of the page
- the section is currently compressed by lower-value neighbors

## Demote

- the section is useful but not needed immediately
- the section is support metadata, not the reason the page exists

## Merge

- the section repeats another section's summary
- the section only makes sense when read together with another block

## Remove

- the section has no real job
- the section exists only as layout filler
- the section title promises value but the content is trivial
